Take your course anywhere

There are many pocket learning apps to teach you all sorts of things. However none of them teach you things that fit the specific curriculum you are already on.

You might be in school, in university getting a degree or taking some online course. The idea of ficka.io is to give you a mobile first, modern interface, to practice things relevant to the learning plan made by your teacher or institution.

Right now, you can use it to practice Swedish words for your A1-A2 course at Folkuniversitetet. Soon, the platform might grow to a variety of subjects, and make use of games and algorithms to support better learning.
